3/22/2025 - 2025 Career Fair

By Angel City Football Club March 22, 2025

DATE: Saturday, March 22, 2025
LOCATION: PUENTE Learning Center
ADDRESS: 501 South Boyle Avenue Los Angeles, CA 90033
START TIME: 9:30 AM
END TIME: 12:30 AM
Join us for our annual 2025 Career Fair on Saturday, March 22, 2025. This is a fantastic opportunity for high school youth and current college students to connect with working professionals and explore exciting career options.

Students will have the chance to attend a career panel discussions and meet with representatives from a wide range of industries, Law, Media, Business, and more! Discover professionnal opportunities, network with professionals, and gain valuable insights into various industries.

Open to all 9th - 12th grade high school students and current college students. Breakfast will be provided, and raffles for both students and parents!
